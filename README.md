# Quantum Learning Theory Notes

This repository contains my structured research notes on the mathematical foundations of Quantum Machine Learning.

## Focus Areas

- Classical Statistical Learning Theory
- VC Dimension and Sauer’s Lemma
- Generalization Bounds
- Towards Quantum VC Dimension
- Geometry of Quantum Hypothesis Classes

## Objective

To develop a rigorous theoretical framework for Quantum Learning Theory, bridging topology, learning theory, and quantum models.
